questions

Before you begin.

Is it really free?+

Yes. The practice, all six lessons, the teacher, the gongs, your progress — free, with no ads. That's how a meditation practice should arrive.

I've never meditated. Can I start here?+

This is exactly who the app is for. The six lessons assume nothing, teach everything, and the technique itself is effortless — if you can think a thought, you can do this.

What kind of meditation is this?+

Effortless Vedic mantra meditation — an ancient practice where a soft inner sound lets the mind settle on its own. No concentration, no breath control, no effort. Taught simply and independently: no courses to buy, no organisation to join.

What if my mind won't stop thinking?+

Then you're doing it right. Thoughts are part of the process, not a failure. You notice, you gently favour the mantra again, and that quiet returning is the whole skill.

How much time does it take?+

Twenty minutes, twice a day, sitting comfortably with your eyes closed. No cushion, no posture, no incense required. A chair is fine.

Where can I get it?+

On Google Play for Android, free. Sit down, close your eyes, and start.
